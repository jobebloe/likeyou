Vision

We believe the next wave of social technology will be quiet, ambient, and respectful. likeyou aims to become the simplest way to notice the people around you who resonate with who you are — without feeds, notifications, or constant screens. We want to connect people in a time where most connections start on screens. likeyou creates quiet, physical signals that help you notice the humans around you again, not just their profiles. We believe that even in a hyper‑online world, people still feel lonely – and that a small glow at the right moment can be enough to start something real.
